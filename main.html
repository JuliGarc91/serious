Phishing Scam Prevention Tips:

  • Be Skeptical of Emails and Messages:
    • Verify the sender's email address before clicking on any links.
    • Be cautious of unexpected emails, especially those asking for personal or financial information.
  • Check for Spelling and Grammar:
    • Phishing emails often contain spelling and grammatical errors. Be wary of such mistakes.
  • Verify Website URLs:
    • Hover over links to see the actual URL before clicking. Ensure they match the legitimate website's address.

Remember that staying vigilant and being cautious in your online activities can significantly reduce the risk of falling victim to phishing scams.
